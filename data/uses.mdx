# Vinyls

- [Discog](https://www.discogs.com/user/rohitjmathew/collection): Tracks all my vinyl purchases
- [Audio-Technica AT-LP60X](https://www.amazon.in/Audio-Technica-Automatic-Belt-Drive-Turntable-Cartridge/dp/B0BTVZ14BH/): One of the best entry level turntables
- **Archival Quality Inner Sleeves**: Much better than rice paper inner sleeves you get with records
- **Outer Sleeves**: To ensure your gatefolds and LPS are protect against the elements and last longer
- [Hudson HiFi 9 in 1 Record Cleaning Kit](https://hudsonhifi.com/collections/lp-cleaning/products/prokit): You really need the anti static and velvet brush to ensure your records are clean. Rest are good to have.
- [Hudson Hifi SpaceBen 8](https://hudsonhifi.com/collections/record-stabilization/products/spacerecordweight): A record weight to enable better tracking, especially on slightly warped records
- [Hudson HiFi Acrylic Turntable Mat](https://hudsonhifi.com/collections/high-fidelity/products/solidslipmat): Changed the standard mat with this for less static and better grip with the record
